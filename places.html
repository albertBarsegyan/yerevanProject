District Of Yerevan

Yerevan is divided into twelve administrative districts each with an elected leader. The total area of the 12 districts of Yerevan is 223 square kilometres (86 square miles)

The twelve districts of Yerevan

Since the 17th century, without the fortress and nearby villages Yerevan was divided into three main quarters Shahar (The Old City), Demir-Bulagh (Karahank) Kond (Tapabash). The market (Ghantar) was separate, between Kond and Shahar.
